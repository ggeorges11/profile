Career Biography

Gilles Georges is a Franco-American technology and business executive who has spent 25 years turning complex science and emerging technologies into operational realities for global industrial companies. His career has taken him from materials research laboratories to the executive suites of 3M, DuPont/Axalta, Milacron, and the American Chemical Society, leading businesses and teams across three continents.

A native of France with a PhD in Chemistry and an MBA from McGill, Gilles began his career qualifying structural composites for aerospace programs at Boeing, Lockheed Martin, and Bell Helicopter, then developing specialty chemicals for oil and gas recovery. This foundation in applied science shaped his approach: always grounded in physical systems, production constraints, and measurable outcomes.

Over two decades, he moved into general management and digital transformation — commercializing optical technologies across Asia-Pacific at 3M ($30M+ new sales, alliances with Sony, Apple, Dolby), leading a $150M business turnaround at Axalta, running a $95M industrial fluids division at Milacron across five countries, and modernizing one of the world's largest scientific AI platforms at CAS.

In 2024, he founded ClarityPoint Solutions to focus on what he sees as the defining challenge for industrial companies: making machine intelligence work — not just as a pilot, but as a sustained operational capability.

Gilles is bilingual in English and French with working knowledge of German and Spanish. A licensed private pilot for both airplanes and helicopters, he brings the same discipline and systems thinking to his advisory work that these pursuits demand.
